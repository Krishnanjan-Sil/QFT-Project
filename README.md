## 📚 Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive reading project report that traces the journey from the fundamental building blocks of quantum mechanics to the frontiers of the Standard Model and beyond. The project synthesizes three major literature reviews:

1. **"Particles and Fields"** - Comprehensive review of Standard Model physics and cosmological connections
2. **"Higgs Physics"** - John Ellis (2013 Lectures) on the theoretical foundations, discovery, and future prospects
3. **"Neutrino Oscillations"** - Bellini, Ludhova, Ranucci & Villante (2014) on mixing, matter effects, and experimental saga
